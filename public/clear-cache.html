Clear Browser Cache

Click the button below to clear all caches and reload the page:

Manual Steps:

  1. Hard Refresh: Press Cmd + Shift + R (Mac) or Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows)
  2. Or: Open DevTools (F12) → Application tab → Clear Storage → Clear site data
  3. Or: Use Incognito/Private window

Dashboard PWA: After clearing, you may need to sign in again to open the dashboard.
